OUR DEVELOPMENT TEAM

We're a small but dedicated team of game enthusiasts based in Belgium, united by our love for pixel art and strategic gameplay. Each member brings unique skills to create an unforgettable gaming experience.

DPB

Diego Portillo Beckand

Head of Programming

As the Head of Programming, I bring extensive experience in game development and will be responsible for both the website and the game itself, utilizing Unity to create an engaging experience for players.

LB

Levi Boons

Head of 3D Models

Levi is in charge of creating and implementing 3D models in the game. His expertise ensures that every model is accurately placed to enhance the overall gameplay experience.
