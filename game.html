Game Concept Art

Strategic castle defense meets farming simulation

DEFEND YOUR KINGDOM

As the count of a castle under constant threat, you must balance resource management, defense strategies, and exploration to survive increasingly dangerous attacks.

Discover the mysterious origins of the attacks while building your kingdom and uncovering secrets in the surrounding lands.

Core Experience

  • Strategic base defense with upgradable fortifications
  • Resource management through farming and gathering
  • Troop training and tactical combat
  • Dynamic day/night cycle affecting gameplay
  • Progression system with kingdom expansion

GAMEPLAY LOOP

Grow & Gather

Cultivate crops, collect resources, and manage your kingdom's economy during peaceful periods.

Build & Upgrade

Strengthen defenses, train troops, and research new technologies to prepare for attacks.

Defend

Command your forces during enemy waves, utilizing strategic placement and special abilities.

Explore & Expand

Discover new areas, uncover secrets, and expand your territory beyond the castle walls.
