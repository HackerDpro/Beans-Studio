SHIELDS & TREBUCHETS

Defend your kingdom, grow your crops, and uncover ancient mysteries in our upcoming pixel-art strategy adventure

Game Details Meet Our Team

GAME FEATURES

Strategic Base Defense

Build and upgrade your castle defenses with walls, towers, and traps to withstand waves of enemies.

Resource Management

Grow crops, gather resources, and manage your kingdom's economy to survive through changing seasons.

Dynamic Environment

Experience a day/night cycle and weather system that affects gameplay mechanics and strategy.

Kingdom Exploration

Discover new areas, uncover secrets, and expand your territory beyond the castle walls.

Beanpedia System

Collect information about enemies, resources, and game lore in your expanding encyclopedia.

Challenges & Events

Participate in seasonal events, complete achievements, and face special challenges.

STRATEGIC KINGDOM DEFENSE

As the count of a castle under constant threat, you must balance resource management, defense strategies, and exploration to survive increasingly dangerous attacks.

Discover the mysterious origins of the attacks while building your kingdom and uncovering secrets in the surrounding lands.
